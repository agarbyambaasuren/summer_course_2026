Welcome to My Exercise Plan!

Are you ready to get started on your fitness journey?
This simple exercise plan will help you build a foundation
for a healthier and more active lifestyle.

Day 1: Walking


Start with a 15-minute brisk walk around your neighborhood
or a nearby park. Focus on maintaining good posture and breathing
rhythmically.

Day 2: Stretching


Perform a 10-minute stretching routine to improve flexibility.
Include stretches for your neck, shoulders, arms, legs, and back.

Day 3: Squats


Do 3 sets of 10 squats. Squats are great for building lower body strength.
Keep your back straight, and go as low as you comfortably can.

Day 4: Rest


Rest is essential for muscle recovery. Take a break today and focus on
staying hydrated.

Day 5: Push-ups


Perform 3 sets of 5 push-ups. If regular push-ups are too challenging,
start with knee push-ups and work your way up.

Day 6: Jumping Jacks


Do 3 sets of 15 jumping jacks. This is a great way to improve
cardiovascular
fitness and burn calories.


Day 7: Rest


Another day of rest to allow your body to recover. Focus on healthy eating
and staying active by walking or stretching.

Remember:


Stay hydrated throughout your workouts.
Listen to your body; don't push too hard, especially if you're just
starting out.
Consistency is key. Try to stick to your exercise plan and
gradually increase intensity as you become more comfortable.
Consult with a healthcare professional before beginning any
new exercise program, especially if you have any underlying health
conditions.
